Empowering AI agents to browse the web for you.

Gobii is an AI agent platform for teams with real work to do.

Our agents are virtual coworkers: they can browse the web, gather research, organize data, complete forms, use connected apps, and deliver reports. You can chat with them, email them, or text them, and they keep working in the background while you focus on higher-value decisions.

We believe AI agents should not just answer questions. They should help move work forward.

That means giving agents the infrastructure they need to operate reliably: cloud-hosted browsers, persistent memory, built-in tools, structured data, integrations, live previews, audit trails, and guardrails for safe execution. Gobii turns web-based work into something you can delegate.

Today, teams use Gobii for recruiting, sales research, operations, competitive intelligence, compliance monitoring, and other workflows where the work is important but painfully repetitive.

Our vision is simple: humans and AI agents working together, each doing what they do best.

If you believe the future of work includes always-on AI coworkers, we’d love to hear from you.
