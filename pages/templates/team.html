Andrew I. Christianson

Founder

Startup founder and growth hacker with the engineering chops to back it up. Andrew has a gift for building audiences and communities fast—and the technical depth to ship the products they're waiting for. He's grown open-source projects 9× in 90 days, built viral social followings from scratch, and brings a decade of battle-tested systems engineering to make sure the infrastructure can keep up.

Creator of RA.Aid, the open-source AI coding agent. Selected as the inaugural project for Open Core Ventures' Catalyst program and drove 9× community growth in 90 days through hands-on developer marketing and community engagement.

Built a 30k+ combined following on TikTok and Instagram in under 30 days, publishing AI and technical content under his initials "AI" Christianson.

Nearly a decade contracting for the U.S. National Security Agency, architecting self-healing distributed computing and big-data pipelines that directly contributed to saving lives and keeping Americans safe.

Core contributor to Apache NiFi & MiNiFi edge-computing—technology originally open-sourced through the NSA Technology Transfer Program. NiFi became the open core behind Onyara (acquired by Hortonworks) and Datavolo (acquired by Snowflake).

Staff Software Engineer at open-core leaders Hortonworks, Cloudera, and FOSSA.

{# Will #}

Will Bonde

Growth & Engineering

Will fuses nearly twenty years of secure‑systems expertise with a knack for turning cutting‑edge AI into products people actually use. A cancer survivor, he channels that resilience into building software with real‑world impact to make lives better—now charting the technical course for Gobii's platform that distills signal from the deluge of information on the internet.

Integration mastermind for high‑security workflows. Led architecture and hands‑on code at Yakabod (acquired by EverFox), wiring ServiceNow, Microsoft Teams, Slack, and classified DoD systems into a self‑healing platform used across multiple secure enclaves.

Creator of self‑documenting REST APIs. Designed OpenAPI‑driven services whose endpoints automatically expand and adjust to object configurations on the server, eliminating manual spec drift and accelerating client adoption.

Architect and implementer of large‑scale data synchronization. Built a system that syncs millions of legacy‑formatted records across agencies to a modern web stack multiple times per hour, ensuring fresh data without downtime.

Co‑creator of Therality, a self‑guided exposure‑therapy app that leverages immersive video to help users tame anxiety. Therality was one of the first applications available on the visionOS store.

Maintainer of RA.Aid, the AI coding agent created by Andrew; streamlined Homebrew packaging to slash installation complexity for macOS users

Contributor to browser-use

Off the command line, Will supports fellow survivors through the Testicular Cancer Foundation, facilitating patient and survivor conversation through Discord.
