Your Agents Just Got a Paper Trail 📎
Master your documents with bidirectional file attachments

We’re kicking off 2026 with one of our biggest updates yet:

Gobii agents now support *bidirectional file attachments*.

That means your agents can now:
- Read files you send them (PDFs, CSVs, images, office docs)
- Analyze and transform that data
- Create new files and send them back to you

Think of it as giving your agents “eyes” to read documents and “hands” to turn them into clean, actionable output.

## As Simple As Forwarding An Email

You can send files to your agent in two ways:

1) Forward an email
Every agent has its own private email address.
Just forward an attachment directly to the agent.

2) Drag and drop
Drop files straight into the web chat and start the analysis instantly.

Your agent will process the file, run its internal logic and live research, and send the results right back to you.
