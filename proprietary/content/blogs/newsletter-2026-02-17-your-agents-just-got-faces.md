## Why It Matters

### One agent

Your agent gets its own personality from the start.

### Five agents

No more squinting at names in a list. Spot the right one instantly.

### Ten agents

When your workspace has multiple specialized agents, visual identity makes it much easier to find the right one quickly.

### Automatic

Every new agent gets a unique avatar the moment you create it. No setup, no picking colors, no uploading icons.

### Distinct

Each avatar is visually unique — designed so your brain can tell agents apart without reading a single word.

### Instant

It just works. Open your console and you will see them right away.
