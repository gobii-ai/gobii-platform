Agents accomplish more when the right people can join the conversation at the right time.

Gobii now makes agent collaboration feel more like adding someone to a group chat. Instead of juggling approval links and back-and-forth setup, you can invite collaborators directly from the chat experience.

## Collaboration In Chat

Adding someone to your agent used to mean approval links and extra coordination. Now it is as simple as clicking "Collaborate" in the upper right of your chat.

## When It Helps

- Bring a teammate into a research workflow.
- Add a customer or partner when the agent needs input.
- Let multiple people monitor a long-running agent task.
- Keep decisions and context in the same conversation.

Agent work is more useful when collaboration happens where the work already lives.
