## Dedicated IPs for Predictable Access

Dedicated IPs are here — for predictable, secure access.

Some teams need more than flexibility — they need consistency and control.

Starting today, Pro and Organization users can add a Dedicated IP to their agents. Each Dedicated IP gives your agent a stable, exclusive address for all outbound connections — ideal when you need:

- Consistent access for critical integrations
- Firewall and allowlist compatibility
- Better control and visibility into network behavior

## How To Enable It

1. Go to Billing
2. Open your Agent's Configuration page

[Add a Dedicated IP](https://gobii.ai/console/billing/?utm_source=blog&utm_medium=web&utm_campaign=dedip1_pro&utm_content=text-cta)

[Then assign it](https://gobii.ai/console/agents/?utm_source=blog&utm_medium=web&utm_campaign=dedip1_pro&utm_content=text-cta2)
