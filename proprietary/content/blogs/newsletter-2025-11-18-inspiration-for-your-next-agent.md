The versatility of Gobii agents continues to grow. Based on recent feedback and stories from the community, here are two workflows that can inspire your next project.

## Creator Autopilots

Some users are running comprehensive creative workflows entirely inside Gobii. These agents handle the repetitive tasks of content production, including:

- Drafting: scripting content and prepping captions/titles
- Quality Control: performing automated QA checks
- Distribution: scheduling posts across multiple platforms

The concept: Think of it as an "autopilot" for niche content—whether that's cooking videos, tech explainers, or educational threads. These agents run on a schedule, keeping the publishing queue full so you can focus on high-level strategy.

[Try a creator workflow](https://gobii.ai/g/lLU/)

## Inventory Monitors

Another popular use case involves setting up a "watchdog" for scarce items. This setup is ideal for tracking:

- Limited-edition drops
- Hard-to-find SKUs
- Volatile pricing on tech or collectibles

Users configure agents to check specific storefronts and trigger an SMS or email the moment stock appears or a price drops. It offers the advantage of a personal shopper that runs 24/7.

[Try an inventory monitor](https://gobii.ai/g/zhe/)
