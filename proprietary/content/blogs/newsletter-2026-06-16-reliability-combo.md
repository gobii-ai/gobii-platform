Your agents just got faster, smoother, and more dependable.

You probably did not notice. That is the point.

This week we shipped over a dozen improvements. None of them make the demo reel. But they are the difference between an agent you manage and an agent you trust.

## Agents Launch The Moment You Need Them

No more staring at a loading spinner while your workspace spins up. Agents are ready when you are.

## Gobii Feels Snappier On Every Device

Check on your agents from your phone without the lag. Review long conversations without fighting the scrollbar.

## Agents Pause Gracefully And Wake Up On Time

Agents now handle limits more gracefully. They pause when needed, resume on schedule, and send one clear update when they are ready.

That means fewer repeat notifications and less noise when an agent is waiting for its next run.

## More Dependable Delivery And File Handling

We improved how agents handle messages, files, exports, and reports.

Agents now verify more steps behind the scenes, recover more cleanly when something needs attention, and do a better job making sure your artifacts end up where they are supposed to be.

## Boringly Dependable Is The Goal

The net effect: your agents run for months without breaking a sweat. No stalls. No loops. No surprises.

Most AI platforms chase flashy demos. We spent this week making Gobii agents boringly dependable. For anyone running AI in production, that is the most exciting thing we could ship.
