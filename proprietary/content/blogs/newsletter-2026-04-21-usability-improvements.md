You asked, we listened.

We know that when you are managing autonomous agents, the little UX details add up fast. Our latest release includes several highly requested quality-of-life updates designed to make your day easier.

## What's New

### Inline approvals

<img src="/static/images/blog/newsletters/newsletter-2026-04-21-inline-approvals.png" alt="Inline approvals in Gobii" style="max-width: 100%; border-radius: 10px;">

When an agent needs you to approve a contact or authorize a secret, you can now handle it right from the new Pending Input Panel. No more losing your place in a complex chat.

### Bulk contact management

<img src="/static/images/blog/newsletters/newsletter-2026-04-21-bulk-contact-management.png" alt="Bulk contact management in Gobii" style="max-width: 100%; border-radius: 10px;">

You can now select and remove multiple contacts at once, because organizing your agent's address book should not be a chore.

### Built from feedback

These updates come directly from your suggestions on how to make managing agents smoother and faster.

Get back to focusing on the big picture, without the friction.
