Interacting with your AI agents should feel as natural and transparent as working with a trusted human colleague. But friction often creeps in — whether it's the inability to easily share a quick screenshot, or losing track of exactly what an agent is planning to do next.

Today, we're removing that friction with a massive Quality-of-Life upgrade to the Gobii web interface.

1) Paste files directly into Web Chat

It sounds simple, but it's a huge time-saver: you can now copy and paste screenshots, PDFs, or code snippets directly into the Gobii chat UI. Drop visual context to your agent without ever touching an "Upload" button.

2) Planning Mode Upgrades

We've completely refined the agent planning experience. Now, when an agent embarks on a multi-step task, you get clearer, more intuitive visibility into its action plan, making it easier to course-correct and collaborate.

3) Quality of Life Tweaks

- Unread Indicators: Notice instantly which agents have new messages so you can catch up faster.

- In-Chat Config: Adjust your agent's settings on the fly without having to hop out of the chat page.

- In-Tab Completion Alerts: Get a handy notification when an agent finishes a task while you Gobii open
