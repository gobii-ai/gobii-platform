We're thrilled to announce a major upgrade: Gobii agents now run persistently, 24/7, and have their own identities (name and email). Even better, they're fully accessible via email, making it effortless for non-technical users to interact and manage tasks directly from their inbox. So try finding some
weekend activities, a job search, or shopping deals.

## What's New

### Agents Work While You Sleep

Your Gobii agents never sleep. They can keep working in the background, even when you're away.

### Personalized Identity

Agents now have unique names and email addresses, which makes them easier to contact and collaborate with.

### Email Integration

You can control and collaborate with your agents through ordinary email. No technical setup required.

SMS, Slack, and Discord support are coming soon.

## Example Use Cases

### Sales Outreach

Find individuals who may be interested in your products or services.

### Shopping

Research the best products, see what real users think, or find out when there's a deal that matches your needs.

### Everyday Tasks

Get updates on your area, the topics you care about, or activities for your family each week.

## Technical Updates

The Gobii API remains stable and reliable. We've also upgraded our backend to the latest browser-use version, alongside significant performance and stability improvements.
