Agent sharing lets you turn a useful Gobii agent into a reusable template.

Send someone a link, and they can spawn their own copy of the agent. They get the structure of the workflow, but your private data stays with you.

## How Agent Sharing Works

When you share an agent, Gobii creates a link that another person can use to create their own version. The recipient gets the same basic setup and workflow pattern, but their copy starts fresh.

That makes it easier to share a helpful automation without exposing the information that made it useful for your own work.

## What Stays Private

- Your contacts stay private.
- Your conversation history stays private.
- Your task data stays private.
- The recipient gets the blueprint, not your business.

## Why It Matters

Many agent workflows are valuable because of their structure: the instructions, the process, the prompts, and the way the agent approaches a recurring job. Sharing lets you pass along that structure without bundling up sensitive context.

It is useful for teammates, customers, friends, or anyone who wants to start from a working pattern instead of a blank prompt.

## Built-In Reward

Every time someone spawns their own agent from your shared template, you earn 100 task credits.
