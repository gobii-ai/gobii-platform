## MCP Support Is Live

Big news - Gobii now supports MCP (Model Context Protocol)!

Your agents can now plug directly into MCP-compatible tools and ecosystems, unlocking richer context, smarter actions, and tighter integrations than ever before.

This is our first release, and we'd love for you to try it out and share feedback. Your input will help guide where we take it next.

## Dashboard Improvements

We also added a few quality-of-life upgrades you'll notice right away:

- Auto summaries: Each agent now has an LLM-generated summary in your dashboard.
- Auto tagging: Agents organize themselves automatically with smart tags.
- Searchable agents: Quickly find what you're looking for.

Check it out here: https://gobii.ai/console/ - Just click on advanced

Thanks for helping us push what agents can do.
