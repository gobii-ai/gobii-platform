Connect Gobii agents directly with Google Sheets—no more copy-paste.

## Google Sheets Integration

Your agents can now connect directly with Google Sheets.
That means no more tedious copy-pasting, CSV wrangling, or version chaos—
just seamless data in and out of your favorite spreadsheet.

## Use It To

- Keep live task results flowing into a shared Sheet.
- Build reports and dashboards in real time.
- Share agent output with your team instantly.

This unlocks a new level of workflow automation—your Gobii agents can now
read, update, and collaborate right from Sheets.

[Connect Google Sheets](https://gobii.ai/g/5n7/)

Available to all Gobii users starting today.
