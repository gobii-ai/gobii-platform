## Agent Skills

The best employees learn on the job.
Now your AI agents do too.

If you've ever felt the frustration of explaining the exact same 5-step workflow to an AI day after day, you're going to love this.

Gobii agents now automatically recognize your repeated workflows and save them as reusable Agent Skills. There is zero setup required. No manual configuration. The agent simply notices the pattern, creates the skill itself, and has it ready for future work.

## Example

If your agent processes weekly reports using the same analysis steps every Monday, it learns that pattern. Next week? It just does it — perfectly consistently — without you having to re-explain the rules.

## Why It Matters

It eliminates re-learning and ensures absolute consistency across runs. Your agent actually gets better at its job over time.
