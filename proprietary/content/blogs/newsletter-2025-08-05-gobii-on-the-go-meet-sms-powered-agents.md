We’ve just made staying in touch with your Gobii agents even easier -
you can now chat with them over SMS
.

## Why You'll Love It

### Immediate reach

Fire off a quick text to kick off a task, check status, or tweak settings. No laptop or app required.

### 24/7 availability

Your always-on agents keep working. Now you can, too, whether you're in line for coffee or on a red-eye flight.

### Friction-free collaboration

Forward info from colleagues or customers straight to an agent and let it handle the follow-up automatically.

## How It Works

1. Head over to [gobii.ai](https://gobii.ai/?utm_source=blog&utm_medium=web).
2. Type in a quick note on what your agent can help you with, then hit "Create Agent."
3. Turn on SMS on the next screen. We'll send a one-time code to confirm it's you.
4. Start texting your agents at the dedicated number we assign.

That’s it. Standard message rates apply, and SMS activity counts toward your normal task credits—no extra fees.

Ready to try? Visit [gobii.ai](https://gobii.ai/?utm_source=blog&utm_medium=web).

Pro-tip: Tell your agent if you need them to answer in a different way - talk to them like a real assistant!
