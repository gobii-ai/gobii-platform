Most AI agents forget.

Send 100 messages. By message 50, the early ones may be gone. By message 80, the agent may be guessing. By message 100, it can feel like starting over with a thin summary of the past.

It's called context rot — and it's the reason most AI agents feel dumber the longer you use them.

Your Gobii doesn't have this problem anymore.

## Infinite Context Recall

With Infinite Context Recall, your agent can reach back to the very first message you ever sent it — and everything in between. Every instruction. Every preference. Every correction. Every conversation.

Not a summary. Not a "best effort." The actual history.

## Why It Matters

### Less agent drift

Your agent doesn't slowly "forget" how you like things done. The tone, format, and preferences you set on day one still hold on day 100.

### Compounding knowledge

The more you work with your agent, the smarter it gets, because it remembers what you have taught it. That offhand correction from three weeks ago is still available.

### Less re-explaining

"I already told you this" is something you should not have to say again. Your agent knows what you told it.

### More trust over time

When your agent remembers your context, you can stop second-guessing whether the old details survived.

Most AI tools get worse the more you use them. Context windows fill up, old messages get dropped, and you're back to square one.

Gobii agents get better the more you use them. Because they never forget.
