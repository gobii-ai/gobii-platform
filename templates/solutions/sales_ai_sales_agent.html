What it does

Give the AI teammate a defined sales workflow

The best AI sales agent work is concrete. Give Gobii the inputs, approved sources, output format, schedule, and review points. The agent handles the repetitive browser and data work around your pipeline.

Prospecting

Turn ICP criteria into recurring searches across approved sources. Collect accounts and contacts that look like a match.

Buying guide

Which type of AI sales agent should you buy?

Different products use the same label. Start with the job: CRM upkeep, outbound volume, calls, or source-linked research a seller can review before anything reaches a buyer.

CRM-native agents

Best for record updates, activity summaries, and account scoring inside Salesforce, HubSpot, or another CRM. Gobii can prepare the research and fields before the handoff.

Outbound BDR agents

Best for high-volume prospecting and sequencing. Gobii fits when you want review-ready lead research and message angles, not unreviewed auto-sending.

Voice and calling agents

Best for phone qualification, call routing, or meeting booking. This Gobii workflow is not an autonomous caller or WhatsApp sender. It prepares briefs and handoff data for approved tools.

Research workflow agents

Best for browsing approved sources, enriching leads, filling sheets, triggering webhooks, and producing source-linked account briefs. Lead Hunter starts here.

Workflow ownership

Use an AI sales employee where repetition slows the team down

Gobii is not a black-box replacement for sales judgment. It is a persistent AI teammate for research and preparation, so sellers can spend more time where context and trust matter.

Inputs

ICP criteria, priority accounts, connected sheets, source lists, CRM exports, market notes, and past lead feedback.

Actions

Search sources, inspect pages, enrich records, compare fit, summarize findings, and prepare review-ready outputs.

Outputs

Lead lists, account briefs, source URLs, fit notes, enrichment fields, outreach drafts, and handoff summaries.

Review

Sales or RevOps checks fit, verifies sources, edits messaging, and decides which prospects should enter active outreach.

Where it fits

Automate the middle of sales research, not the human relationship

Sales teams already have databases, CRMs, sequencing tools, and call tools. The slow work sits between them: finding context, checking fit, cleaning records, and keeping handoffs current. That is where an AI sales agent helps.
