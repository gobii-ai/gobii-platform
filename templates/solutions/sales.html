Questions

How do Gobii sales agents work?

Gobii agents are AI-powered workers that browse the web and complete tasks like a human would. For sales, they search LinkedIn, company databases, and industry sources to find prospects matching your ICP, then deliver organized results to your CRM or preferred destination.

Can I customize the targeting criteria?

Yes. Define your ideal customer profile in plain language. Specify industry, company size, job titles, tech stack, funding stage, or any other criteria. Your agent adapts to your exact requirements.

Does this integrate with my CRM?

Gobii agents export to Google Sheets, CSV, or push data via webhooks. Direct integrations with Salesforce, HubSpot, and other popular CRMs are available for enterprise customers.

How is this different from a lead database?

Lead databases give you static lists. Gobii agents actively search the web in real-time, finding fresh prospects and enriching them with current information. They work like an SDR, not a spreadsheet.
