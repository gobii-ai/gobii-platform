Questions

How does Gobii support HIPAA compliance?

Gobii is open source and designed to support HIPAA compliance. Your security team can audit the code, and you self-host in your own environment to maintain full control of patient data. We provide BAA support and help configure agents with encryption, audit trails, and role-based access control.

Does Gobii integrate with my EHR?

Yes. Gobii agents integrate with major EHR systems including Epic, Cerner, athenahealth, eClinicalWorks, and others. We work with your IT team to ensure secure, compliant data flow.

Can Gobii handle prior authorizations?

Yes. Our Prior Auth Agent automates the submission process, tracks status across payer portals, and flags issues before they become denials. Many practices see 50%+ reduction in auth-related staff time.

Why is Gobii open source?

Healthcare organizations need to trust the software handling patient data. Open source means your security and compliance teams can audit every line of code. You can self-host in your own environment, ensuring data never leaves your infrastructure. No black boxes, no vendor lock-in.
