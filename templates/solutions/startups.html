Questions

Is Gobii meant to replace hiring?

No. The startup use case is about work that would otherwise go unowned because an early-stage team cannot justify the hire yet. Gobii gives those workflows an operator until the company is ready to hire.

Which startup workflows should we delegate first?

Start with high-frequency work that needs research, monitoring, list building, or structured output: outbound prospecting, competitor tracking, weekly finance ops, customer follow-up, candidate sourcing, and investor research.

Can agents connect to Stripe and Brex?

Yes. Startup agents can use connected systems like Stripe and Brex to watch revenue, spend, vendors, failed payments, renewal dates, and cash-impacting changes, then report back through Slack, Sheets, or email.

Do founders approve work before it goes out?

You decide the workflow. Agents can draft messages, prepare lists, flag issues, and ask for approval before outreach, financial decisions, customer replies, or hiring actions.

What plan fits an early-stage startup?

Most lean teams can start with Pro, then move to Scale when agents are running more frequent workflows, higher volumes, or multiple functions at once.
