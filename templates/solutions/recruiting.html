Frequently asked questions

How do Gobii recruiting agents work?

Gobii agents are AI-powered digital workers that browse the web, extract information, and complete tasks just like a human would. For recruiting, they can search job boards, LinkedIn, GitHub, and other platforms to find candidates matching your criteria, then deliver organized results to your preferred destination.

Can I customize the search criteria?

Absolutely. You define your ideal candidate profile in plain language. Specify skills, years of experience, location preferences, company backgrounds, education, or any other criteria that matters for your role. Your agent adapts to your exact requirements.

Does this integrate with my ATS?

Gobii agents can export results to Google Sheets, CSV files, or push data to your existing tools via webhooks and integrations. For popular ATS platforms, we can configure direct integrations on Scale plans.

What's the difference between Pro and Scale?

Pro is perfect for growing teams hiring across multiple roles. Scale is designed for high-volume recruiting operations that need more agents, higher task limits, priority support, and custom integrations. Visit our pricing page for a full comparison.
