Questions

How do Gobii recruiting agents work?

Gobii agents are AI-powered workers that browse the web and complete tasks like a human would. For recruiting, they search job boards, LinkedIn, GitHub, and other platforms to find candidates matching your criteria, then deliver organized results to your preferred destination.

Can I customize the search criteria?

Yes. Define your ideal candidate profile in plain language. Specify skills, experience, location, company backgrounds, education, or any other criteria. Your agent adapts to your exact requirements.

Does this integrate with my ATS?

Gobii agents export to Google Sheets, CSV, or push data via webhooks. Direct integrations with popular ATS platforms are available on Scale plans.

What's the difference between Pro and Scale?

Pro works well for growing teams hiring across multiple roles. Scale is for high-volume operations needing more agents, higher limits, priority support, and custom integrations.
