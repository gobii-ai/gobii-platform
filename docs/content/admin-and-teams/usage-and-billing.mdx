Billing and usage determine how much work your Gobiis can perform and where that work is charged.

Open:

- Billing: `https://gobii.ai/console/billing/`
- Usage: `https://gobii.ai/console/usage/`

## Billing controls

Depending on plan and role, billing can include:

- Current plan.
- Seats for organizations.
- Task packs or additional task behavior.
- Contact packs.
- Dedicated IP add-ons.
- Advanced CAPTCHA or other add-ons where available.
- Stripe billing portal actions for supported accounts.

## Additional tasks

Additional task controls decide what happens after included usage is exhausted:

- Stop at included limits.
- Allow a capped amount of extra usage.
- Allow unlimited additional usage where supported.

Use caps for early workflows until you understand burn rate.

## Usage views

Usage reporting can help answer:

- Which Gobiis are consuming the most credits?
- Which tools are driving usage?
- Is burn rate rising?
- Are API calls or persistent steps a meaningful share of usage?
- Did deleted or archived Gobiis contribute to historical usage?

Use usage views after creating new recurring Gobiis or enabling broad tools.

## Organization billing

Organization billing is separate from personal billing. Confirm the active context before changing plan, seats, add-ons, or additional task settings.

See [Organizations And Seats](/admin-and-teams/organizations-and-seats).
