Organizations let teams share agents, credits, and management responsibilities.

## What organizations include

- Shared agents
- Shared task credit usage
- Team member and invite management
- Seat-based billing controls

## Typical organization actions

- Create or join an organization
- Invite members
- Update member roles
- Remove members
- Leave an organization
- Manage seat count and seat billing

## Why users may not see certain actions

Some organization controls only appear to users with the correct role. If a user cannot see billing or member controls, check their organization role first.

## Where to manage organizations

- **[Organizations list](https://gobii.ai/console/organizations/)**: View organizations and pending invites.
- **Organization detail**: Open any organization from the Organizations list to manage members, invites, and seat information.
- **[Billing](https://gobii.ai/console/billing/)**: Manage paid seats and seat-related billing changes.
