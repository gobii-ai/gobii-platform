## What is Gobii?

Gobii is an AI Agent platform, providing individuals and organizations with 24/7 AI assistants and AI employees. Collectively, we call them agents or "Gobiis" (get it?)

Users may interact with them across multiple channels such as email, web chat, and SMS. Additionally, developers may use our APIs and webhooks to interact with them programmatically.

## Who is Gobii for?

Gobii is for anyone who needs reliable work done inside a web browser — without doing it manually.

It’s especially helpful for:

### Individuals
- Managing repetitive online workflows
- Scraping or collecting information from websites
- Tracking prices, results, or availability
- Filling out forms or navigating dashboards
- Automating simple “check this, then do that” routines

### Professionals
- Assistants, operations roles, and customer support
- Researchers gathering data or monitoring competitors
- Real estate, recruiting, finance, and e-commerce
- Anyone responsible for ongoing web-based processes
- People who need 24/7 automation without writing code

### Developers
- Building automation into your own apps
- Giving your users AI agents that can act online
- Integrating browser-based workflows into backend jobs
- Orchestrating multi-step tasks through an API

### Teams & Organizations
- Companies centralizing automation under a shared workspace
- Shared agents with audit trails, permissions, and histories
- Scalable task execution for high-volume workflows
- Per-seat access and billing controls

**In short:**
If the work happens in a web browser, Gobii can do it for you — reliably, repeatedly, and at scale.

## Example use cases

1) **Inventory Monitoring**. A popular use case involves setting up a "watchdog" for scarce items, or supplies that have frequently varying prices across vendors. This setup is ideal for tracking:

      - Volatile pricing
      - Hard-to-find SKUs
      - Limited-edition drops

2) **Creator Autopilots**. We’ve heard from users who are running comprehensive creative workflows entirely inside Gobii. These agents handle the repetitive tasks of content production, including:

      - Drafting: Scripting content and prepping captions/titles.
      - Quality Control: Performing automated QA checks.
      - Distribution: Scheduling posts across multiple platforms.
      - The Concept: Think of it as an "autopilot" for niche content—whether that’s cooking videos, tech explainers, or educational threads. These agents run on a schedule, keeping the publishing queue full so you can focus on high-level strategy.
